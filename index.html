Features

Run on Blockchain

The Content Fly platform is run on the decentralized blockchain: Internet Computer, using tamper-proof smart contracts called Canisters. All content is stored in the blockchain including creators’ portfolios, content jobs, images, video, audio, text.. etc.

Smart Contract
Job Payment

Fully autonomous, non-custodial smart contract payment for jobs between buyers and creators, with no middle man

Buyers pay for jobs upfront and the money is stored in the smart contract until the job is complete.

How it works for Content Buyers
01. Create a Job

Create a job for the content required & share the unique job link to your community or individuals, inviting creators to pitch for the job.

02. Collaborate With Creators

Receive pitches through your shared link and the Creators on the Content Fly platform. Choose who to work with and ask for adjustments to be made to the concept.

03. Receive Your Amazing Content

Agree on the final version of the content. Approve the final deliverable file and mint your Deliverable Ownership and Proof of Job Completion NFTs.

How it works for Content Creators
01. Pitch for Jobs

Find jobs on Content Fly or through unique job links. Pitch your idea for the job and include a concept.

02. Undertake Jobs & Get Paid

Once selected for a job, work with the buyer to iterate on your concept until the final version is agreed on. Mint your Proof of Job Completion NFT and receive payment!

03. Add Your Portfolio of Work

If you want to be listed as a creator on Content Fly, notified for matching jobs and invited by buyers to pitch on jobs, make a creator profile and add your portfolio.

Our Vision

Most of the existing job marketplace platforms were created for an old business model where the users’ value is constantly being extracted. Those platforms were built with a top-down corporate culture approach where the revenue of the platform is the number one priority. Their system architecture is based on multiple levels of value extraction where middleman fees are charged at every level of the transaction. By the time the platform creators receive their work payouts it is already hugely diminished. In a nutshell, it is an unfair economy, especially for the younger generation. We believe now is the time for a big change.

We are leveraging the recent advances in blockchain technology to build a whole new job marketplace platform from the ground up, utilising blockchain’s value transfer & settlement layer. Web3 technologies enable a decentralized application to be run by a DAO, made up of the users who own and benefit from interacting and steering the platform.

Unlike older generations who worked in the same job for multiple decades, many of today's digital workers prefer undertaking multiple jobs to increase the diversity of their personal and career experience & growth.

We imagine a future where creators can join the Content Fly DAO and receive dividend style income through platform ownership and income from jobs completed. Creators can switch between different jobs and employment types and take ownership of Content Fly to help it evolve.

The platform itself will also have internal jobs that the users can work on, ensuring it operates as a self-growing platform.

More innovative payment methods, for instance, streaming payment and recurring payment could be developed to facilitate partnerships between buyers and creators.

Creators can also band together as a team to tackle jobs. A globally diverse team from different countries or backgrounds could be formed as part of the job requirements to encourage racial diversity and knowledge sharing.

Content Fly runs on Dfinity's Internet Computer

⊛ Internet Computer is the world's first blockchain that runs at web speed and serves content on the web, with unbounded capacity.

⊛ Internet computer is both permissionless and trustless and has no centralized gatekeeper or cloud service provider.

⊛ The Dfinity organisation is the creator of Internet Computer.

⊛ It completes the Open Web / Web3 paradigm that enables Dapps (decentralized applications such as Content Fly) to be fully decentralized, with the full infrastructure stack running directly on blockchain through smart contracts instead of through traditional cloud services. It is the backbone of fully scalable Open Internet Services.

⊛ By building on the Internet Computer, it allows us to put you, the users, in the front seat and take charge of the future trajectory of Content Fly. Through Content Fly's tokenized economy users will be rewarded for interacting with the platform and determine how Content Fly evolves.

⊛ Unlike other blockchains, Internet Computer has a reverse-gas model, meaning users don’t need to worry about paying for the cost of using the smart contracts that run Dapps.  Internet Computer Canisters are supplied with Cycles (the equivalent of Ethereum gas) by developers when they are deployed to Internet Computer. The cost of running Canisters is much lower than other blockchains as there is no competition to get users’ transactions through to the blockchain.
